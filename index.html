Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need any background knowledge to join this course?

No prior experience is required. The 5Q program is designed for parents, educators, professionals, and anyone interested in personal growth and communication. Our facilitators will guide you step by step.

2. Why do I need an interview before joining?

The interview isn’t a test. It’s a friendly conversation to:

  • Understand your learning goals
  • See how the program can best support you
  • Ensure you feel ready for the journey ahead

This way, every participant starts on the right footing.

3. How long is the course?

Each module has 9 lessons. With 5 modules a year, that’s 45 weeks of learning.
Most students complete 7–12 modules, depending on their pace and goals.

4. How often are classes held?

Classes are once a week, every Thursday. This flexible schedule is designed to fit into busy lives while maintaining consistent progress.

5. What if I miss a class?

Don’t worry — recordings, notes, and make-up options are available so you won’t fall behind.

6.Is this program online or physical?

The program is delivered online with live interactive classes. Some hybrid opportunities may be offered at KL, Penang, or Johor centres.

7. What certificate will I receive?

You’ll receive a Certificate of Completion for the 5Q Quantum Communication and Social Science program accredited by CCE Finland, which also qualifies you for progression into the Professional Diploma in 6Q Quantum Communication and Social Science.

8. How is 5Q different from other communication programs?

Most programs focus on surface techniques. 5Q goes deeper — combining psychology, neuroscience, and social science with Finnish education principles to help you master communication, resilience, and leadership authentically.

9. How do I get started?
